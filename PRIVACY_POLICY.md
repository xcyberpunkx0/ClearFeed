# Privacy Policy for ClearFeed 🛡️

**Last Updated**: March 21, 2026

ClearFeed ("we," "our," or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
ClearFeed **does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information** (PII) to external servers. 
- **User Preferences**: Your settings (e.g., "Focus Mode" toggle, "Hide Shorts" preference) are stored locally on your device via `chrome.storage.local` or synced across your devices via your Google account using `chrome.storage.sync`.
- **Task List**: Any tasks you enter into the integrated dashboard are stored exclusively on your local device.
- **Web Activity**: The Extension only acts on `youtube.com`. It does not track, store, or share your browsing history.

## 2. Data Security
Because all data is stored locally or within your secure browser profile, we do not have access to your information. Your data remains under your control at all times.

## 3. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the Extension's repository or documentation.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out via our GitHub repository or contact email provided in the Chrome Web Store.
